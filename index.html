Five Most Watched Debates in US History

While the U.S. presidential debates in 2024 have been historic, they do not rank amongst the 5 most viewed.

1. The first debate between President Barack Obama and Sen. Mitt Romney garnered 84 million views on Oct. 3rd 2012.

2. The second debate between President Jimmy Carter and then-Gov. Ronald Reagan garnered 80.6 million views on Oct. 28, 1980.
